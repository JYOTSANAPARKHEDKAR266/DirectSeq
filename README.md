# DirectSeq

DirectSeq Biosciences Website

The official website of DirectSeq Biosciences, a leader in next-generation RNA sequencing and RNA therapeutics research.
It presents the company’s mission, innovative DSMX-Seq™ technology, services, leadership team, research collaborations, and key publications in a clear and visually engaging way.

Visitors can explore DirectSeq’s scientific focus, learn about its groundbreaking approach to RNA quality assurance, and connect with the team through an integrated contact form.
